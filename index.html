I Present to you
Antonette Torno!!

Background /Past

Hello I am Antonette I am 32 years old and was born in the Philippines. I came from a small city called Mexico Pampanga which is about a two-hour ride North from Manila the capital city. My mother was a stay at home mom and my father worked as a tractor driver for a farm.

After finishing high school I started to work as a sales clerk at SM Department store. I worked there for 6 months and received a scholarship to learn biology unfortunately I was not able to complete the degree due to lack of finances. Shortly after stopping school I was petitioned by a family member in 2009 to come to USA and moved to Burbank.

Recent/ Present

Shortly after coming to the United States I got a job being a caregiver at a facility taking care of patients that are elderly and or have disabilities. I later got my license to be a CNA (Certified Nursing Assistant). I also got my BLS (Basic Life Support) Cert and HHA (Home Health Aid) Cert.

In 2014 I became a U.S. Citizen and was sworn in during a ceremony in Downtown San Diego. When I video called to tell my mother after the ceremony, she cried. For my spare time I am mostly a home body. However, I enjoy cooking, walking in the park with friends, playing shooting games like call of duty, shopping and watching Netflix. I also like traveling and have gone to the San Diego Zoo X6, Sea Word X8, hiking x10 and Disney Land X5.

Near Future/ Goals

My plans for the future include becoming a Physical Therapy assistant. I plan on attending Concord College. I will need to study more before going there since English is my Second language and while I speak English fluently, I have some work to do with writing. I have chosen Physical Therapy because of its long term career outlook and I like working with people.
